This is the local render backend for Vivijure, a generative image and video engine that runs on
your own hardware. The full, canonical Acceptable Use Policy lives with the Vivijure Studio:
**[Vivijure Acceptable Use Policy](https://github.com/skyphusion-labs/vivijure/blob/main/docs/legal/ACCEPTABLE-USE.md)**.
It applies here too. This file restates the one line that is absolute, because the CSAM bright
line belongs on every product that can generate, whether or not you run the studio.

## The absolute red line: no CSAM, no non-consensual imagery

You may not use this software to create, generate, attempt to generate, train a model for,
store, or distribute **child sexual abuse material (CSAM): any sexual or sexualized depiction
of a minor, real or synthetic.** This is zero-tolerance and has no exceptions: AI-generated,
"fake," fictional, cartoon, drawn, or "pretend" depictions count exactly the same as a
photograph of a real child, and there is no artistic, satirical, or "age-play" exception. The
same prohibition covers **non-consensual intimate imagery (NCII)** and **non-consensual
deepfakes** of real, identifiable people. This is also the law, not only our policy.

Because this runs on your own hardware, you are the operator: Skyphusion Labs hosts nothing,
operates nothing, and architecturally cannot see what you generate. That hands-off posture has
exactly one exception, and this is it. The Vivijure project unequivocally condemns CSAM, and on
any touchpoint the project itself operates it will preserve and report to the National Center
for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC, via its CyberTipline at report.cybertip.org) and to law
enforcement. A private instance is not a safe place to do this, and our privacy stance is not a
shield for it.

For everything else (hate and harassment, fraud, IP infringement, other illegal use, and the
enforcement and reporting details), see the canonical studio AUP linked above.

## Reporting

Suspected CSAM: report directly to NCMEC (report.cybertip.org) and/or law enforcement. Other
abuse involving this project: **abuse@skyphusion.org**.
